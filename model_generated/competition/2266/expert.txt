-----Input----- Input is given from Standard Input in the following format: N M L_1 R_1 : L_N R_N -----Output----- Print the minimum required number of additional chairs. -----Sample Input----- 4 4 0 3 2 3 1 3 3 4 -----Sample Output----- 0 The four people can sit in chairs at the coordinates 3, 2, 1 and 4, respectively, and no more chair is needed.
