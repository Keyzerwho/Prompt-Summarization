 You are given coins $A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H , I, J$. Each coin is unique. You have been sent to a town to collect coins. There are enemies in the town. You have to kill one enemy and get his coin. You have to kill all the enemies in the town and collect their coins. You have to collect all the coins and only then you will get the victory. You are a brave gamer so you took this hard challange and successfully finished it. After finishing, you are thinking of the game. You know the order off collecting coin. Now you are thinking how many enemies did you have killed 
-----Input:----- First line of the input is an integer $T$.Next T line consists of a string which denotes the order of your collecting coins. The string consists of Uppercase latin latter only and from A to J. -----Output:----- Print T line, in each line an integer with the number of enemy you have killed in the operation. -----Constraints----- - $1 \leq T \leq 5000$ -----Sample Input:----- 1 ABCDEFGHIJ -----Sample Output:----- 10
