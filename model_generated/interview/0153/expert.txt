 You are given several matchsticks. You need to output whether you can make one square using all the matchsticks. 
Example 1: Input: [1,1,2,2,2] Output: true Explanation: You can form a square with length 2, one side of the square came two sticks with length 1. Example 2: Input: [3,3,3,3,4] Output: false Explanation: You cannot find a way to form a square with all the matchsticks. Note: The length sum of the given matchsticks is in the range of 0 to 10^9. The length of the given matchstick array will not exceed 15.
