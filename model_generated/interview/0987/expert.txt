 Usain Bolt is racing a tiger. The finishing line is $finish$ meters away from Bolt's starting position. The tiger starts with an initial speed of $0$ $meter/second$, and will accelerate itself with $tigerAccelaration$ $m/s^2$. Bolt can run with a constant speed of $boltSpeed$ $m/s$ from start to finish. Given these values, find out who will win the race - Bolt or the tiger 
-----Input:----- - The first line will contain $T$, the number of testcases. Then the description of each test case follow.  - Each test case contains 4 integers $finish, distancetoBolt, tigerAccelaration, boltSpeed$.  -----Output:----- For each testcase, output in a single line, the word "Bolt" or "Tiger" without quotes, depending on whether Bolt wins or the tiger wins. -----Constraints----- - $1 \leq T \leq 100000$ - $1 \leq finish\leq 10^5$ - $1 \leq distancetoBolt\leq 10^5$ - $1 \leq tigerAccelaration\leq 10^5$ - $1 \leq boltSpeed\leq 10^5$ -----Sample Input:----- 2 10 100 10 10 100 10 5 10 -----Sample Output:----- Bolt Tiger
