 You are given an array days. Each day is an integer from 1 to 365. Train tickets are sold in 3 different ways: a 1-day pass is sold for costs[0] dollars; a 7-day pass is sold for costs[1] dollars; a 30-day pass is sold for costs[2] dollars. The passes allow that many days of consecutive travel.  For example, if we get a 7-day pass on day 2, then we can travel for 7 days: day 2, 3, 4, 5
