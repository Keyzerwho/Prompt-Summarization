 Tic-tac-toe is played by two players A and B on a 3 x 3 grid. Here are the rules of Tic-Tac-Toe: Players take turns placing characters into empty squares (" "). The first player A always places "X" characters, while the second player B always places "O" characters. "X" and "O" characters are always placed into empty squares, never on filled ones.
