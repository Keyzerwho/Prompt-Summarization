 DropCaps means that the first letter of the starting word of the paragraph should be in caps and the remaining lowercase, just like you see in the newspaper.  But for a change, let's do that for each and every word of the given String. Your task is to capitalize every word that has length greater than 2, leaving smaller words as they are. Given a string of characters, your task is to return a new string that, for each character C, has C in upper case and anything else in lower case.
