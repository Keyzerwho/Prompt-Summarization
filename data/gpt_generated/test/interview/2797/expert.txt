 You and GTmac are given a blackboard expression. You have to determine who can evaluate the expression. 
-----Input----- The input has two integers $a$ ($1 \leq a \leq 10^9$) and $b$ ($1\leq b\leq 99$, $b$ is odd) on a single line. -----Output----- Output the value of the expression with the given $a$ and $b$. -----Examples----- Sample Input 1: 2 3 Sample Output 1: 1 Sample Input 2: 3 7 Sample Output 2: 0
