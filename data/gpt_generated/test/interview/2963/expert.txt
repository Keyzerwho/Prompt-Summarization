 You are given a number $n$. Find a number $x$ such that $x^x$ is $n$. 
-----Input----- The first and only input line consists of a single integer $n$, the number Neelix saw on his screen. -----Output----- Output the number $x$, where $x^x = n$. Your answer must have an absolute or relative error of at most $10^{-6}$. -----Limits-----  - $1 \leq n \leq 10000000000$ -----Examples----- Sample Input: 4 Sample Output: 2.0
