 $T_2(n)$ is the number of odd binomial coefficients such that $0 \le k \le m < n$. Emma doesn t like such imprecise inequalities and would like to calculate $T_2(n)$ exactly. Can you help her?
-----Input----- The input contains one line with one integer $n, 1 \leq n \le 10^{11}$. -----Output----- Output one line with the value of $T_2(n)$. -----Examples----- Sample Input: 4 Sample Output: 9
