 Given N integers, find the sum of \gcd(A_1, ..., A_N) over all of them. 
-----Input----- Input is given from Standard Input in the following format: N K -----Output----- Print the sum of \gcd(A_1, ..., A_N) over all K^N sequences, modulo (10^9+7). -----Sample Input----- 3 2 -----Sample Output----- 9 \gcd(1,1,1)+\gcd(1,1,2)+\gcd(1,2,1)+\gcd(1,2,2)+\gcd(2,1,1)+\gcd(2,1,2)+\gcd(2,2,1)+\gcd(2,2,2)=1+1+1+1+1+1+1+2=9 Thus, the answer is 9.
