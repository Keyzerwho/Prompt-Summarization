 You are fighting a monster. You can cast different spells. Cast spells to decrease the monster's health. Win when health becomes 0. 
-----Input----- Input is given from Standard Input in the following format: H N A_1 B_1 : A_N B_N -----Output----- Print the minimum total Magic Points that have to be consumed before winning. -----Sample Input----- 9 3 8 3 4 2 2 1 -----Sample Output----- 4 First, let us cast the first spell to decrease the monster's health by 8, at the cost of 3 Magic Points. The monster's health is now 1. Then, cast the third spell to decrease the monster's health by 2, at the cost of 1 Magic Point. The monster's health is now -1. In this way, we can win at the total cost of 4 Magic Points.
