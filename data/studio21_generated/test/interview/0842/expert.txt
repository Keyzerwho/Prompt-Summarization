 You are given an integer n. Find n-th even-length positive palindrome number. 
-----Input----- The only line of the input contains a single integer n (1 ≤ n ≤ 10^{100 000}). -----Output----- Print the n-th even-length palindrome number. -----Examples----- Input 1 Output 11 Input 10 Output 1001 -----Note----- The first 10 even-length palindrome numbers are 11, 22, 33, ... , 88, 99 and 1001.
