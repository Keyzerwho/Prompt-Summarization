 You are given an integer $n$. You are asked to find the number of games Petya will get a bonus. 
-----Input----- The only line of the input contains one integer n (1 ≤ n ≤ 10^18) — the prediction on the number of people who will buy the game. -----Output----- Output one integer showing how many numbers from 1 to n are not divisible by any number from 2 to 10. -----Examples----- Input 12 Output 2
