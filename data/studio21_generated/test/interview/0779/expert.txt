 You are given n employees. Fafa wants to divide employees into teams. Fafa wants to choose l team leaders in such a way that it is possible to divide employees between them evenly. 
-----Input----- The input consists of a single line containing a positive integer n (2 ≤ n ≤ 10^5) — the number of employees in Fafa's company. -----Output----- Print a single integer representing the answer to the problem. -----Examples----- Input 2 Output 1 Input 10 Output 3 -----Note----- In the second sample Fafa has 3 ways:  choose only 1 employee as a team leader with 9 employees under his responsibility.  choose 2 employees as team leaders with 4 employees under the responsibility of each of them.  choose 5 employees as team leaders with 1 employee under the responsibility of each of them.
