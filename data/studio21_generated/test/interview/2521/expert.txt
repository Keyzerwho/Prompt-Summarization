 You are given an integer N, you need to find the maximum possible score. 
-----Input----- Input is given from Standard Input in the following format: N a_1 a_2 ... a_{3N} -----Output----- Print the maximum possible score of a'. -----Sample Input----- 2 3 1 4 1 5 9 -----Sample Output----- 1 When a_2 and a_6 are removed, a' will be (3, 4, 1, 5), which has a score of (3 + 4) - (1 + 5) = 1.
