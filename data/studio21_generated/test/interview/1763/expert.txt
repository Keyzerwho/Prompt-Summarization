 You are given $N$ positive integers $h_{1}, h_{2}, \dots, h_{N}$. You need to find the minimum total cost of restoration. 
-----Input----- The first line of input contains four integers $N$, $A$, $R$, $M$ ($1 \le N \le 10^{5}$, $0 \le A, R, M \le 10^{4}$) — the number of pillars and the costs of operations. The second line contains $N$ integers $h_{i}$ ($0 \le h_{i} \le 10^{9}$) — initial heights of pillars. -----Output----- Print one integer — the minimal cost of restoration. -----Examples----- Input 3 1 100 100 1 3 8 Output 12 Input 3 100 1 100 1 3 8 Output 9 Input 3 100 100 1 1 3 8 Output 4 Input 5 1 2 4 5 5 3 6 5 Output 4 Input 5 1 2 2 5 5 3 6 5 Output 3
