 Given two sequences of integers, count the number of pairs that satisfy the following conditions:  - A_i ≠ B_i, for every i such that 1 ≤ i ≤ N.  - A_i ≠ A_j and B_i ≠ B_j, for every (i, j) such that 1 ≤ i < j ≤ N. 
-----Input----- Input is given from Standard Input in the following format: N M -----Output----- Print the count modulo (10^9+7). -----Sample Input----- 2 2 -----Sample Output----- 2 A_1=1,A_2=2,B_1=2,B_2=1 and A_1=2,A_2=1,B_1=1,B_2=2 satisfy the conditions.
