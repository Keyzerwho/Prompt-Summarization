 You are given N towns. There is one teleporter in each town. The teleporter in Town i (1 \leq i \leq N) sends you to Town A_i. Takahashi, the king, loves the positive integer K. The selfish king wonders what town he will be in if he starts at Town 1 and uses a teleporter exactly K times from there. 
-----Input----- Input is given from Standard Input in the following format: N K A_1 A_2 \dots A_N -----Output----- Print the integer representing the town the king will be in if he starts at Town 1 and uses a teleporter exactly K times from there. -----Sample Input----- 4 5 3 2 4 1 -----Sample Output----- 4 If we start at Town 1 and use the teleporter 5 times, our travel will be as follows: 1 \to 3 \to 4 \to 1 \to 3 \to 4.
