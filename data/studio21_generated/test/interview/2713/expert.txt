 A player starts at rank $25$ with no stars. Given the match history of a player, what is her rank at the end of the sequence of matches 
-----Input----- The input consists of a single line describing the sequence of matches. Each character corresponds to one game; ‘W’ represents a win and ‘L’ a loss. The length of the line is between $1$ and $10000$ characters (inclusive). -----Output----- Output a single line containing a rank after having played the given sequence of games; either an integer between $1$ and $25$ or “Legend”. -----Examples----- Sample Input 1: WW Sample Output 1: 25 Sample Input 2: WWW Sample Output 2: 24 Sample Input 3: WWWW Sample Output 3: 23 Sample Input 4: WLWLWLWL Sample Output 4: 24 Sample Input 5: WWWWWWWWWLLWW Sample Output 5: 19
