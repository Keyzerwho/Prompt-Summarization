 You are given current year in Berland. Your task is to find how long will residents of Berland wait till the next lucky year. 
-----Input----- The first line contains integer number n (1 ≤ n ≤ 10^9) — current year in Berland. -----Output----- Output amount of years from the current year to the next lucky one. -----Examples----- Input 4 Output 1 Input 201 Output 99 Input 4000 Output 1000 -----Note----- In the first example next lucky year is 5. In the second one — 300. In the third — 5000.
