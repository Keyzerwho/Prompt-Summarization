 You are given four cookies with deliciousness A, B, C, and D. He will choose one or more from those cookies and eat them. Is it possible that the sum of the deliciousness of the eaten cookies is equal to that of the remaining cookies. 
-----Input----- Input is given from Standard Input in the following format: A B C D -----Output----- If it is possible that the sum of the deliciousness of the eaten cookies is equal to that of the remaining cookies, print Yes; otherwise, print No. -----Sample Input----- 1 3 2 4 -----Sample Output----- Yes  - If he eats the cookies with deliciousness 1 and 4, the sum of the deliciousness of the eaten cookies will be equal to that of the remaining cookies.
