 You are given $n$ integers $a_1, a_2, \ldots , a_ n$ ($0 \le a_ i \le m$ for each $i$). You are also given $k$ positive integers. You are asked to find the number of permutations of $1,2, \ldots , n$ so that if the critics arrive in this order the average score will be exactly $k/n$. 
Examples----- Sample Input: 5 10 30 10 5 3 1 3 Sample Output: 3 5 2 1 4
