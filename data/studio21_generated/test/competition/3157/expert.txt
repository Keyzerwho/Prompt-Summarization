 Given an array of integers $X$, your task is to calculate the nth Fibonacci number $F_{n}$. 
-----Input----- The first line of input contains three integers $N$, $K$ and $M$ ($1 \leq N \leq 10$, $0 \leq K < 2^M$, $6 \leq M \leq 25$). -----Output----- The first and only line of output must consist of the required number from the task. -----Examples----- Sample Input 1: 1 0 10 Sample Output 1: 0 Sample Input 2: 1 2 10 Sample Output 2: 1
