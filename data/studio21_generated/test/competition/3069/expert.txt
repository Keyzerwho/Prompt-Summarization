 You are given a sequence of brackets. You need to output whether it is possible to make the sequence valid by performing at most one segment inversion operation. 
-----Input----- The input consists of one line containing between $1$ and $5000$ brackets. -----Output----- Output “possible” if you can make the bracket sequence valid by performing at most one segment inversion, or “impossible” otherwise. -----Examples----- Sample Input 1: ())) Sample Output 1: possible Sample Input 2: )))( Sample Output 2: impossible
