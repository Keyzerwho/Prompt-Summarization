 A frog speaks in a very strange variation of English: it reverses sentence, omitting all punctuation marks except the final exclamation, question or period. We need to build a translator. To simplify the task, we always use lower-case letters. Apostrophes are forbidden as well. Translator should be able to process multiple sentences in one go. Sentences are separated by arbitrary amount of spaces. **Examples** `you should use python.` -> `python use should you.` `look, a fly!` -> `fly a look!` `multisentence is good. is not it ` -> `good
