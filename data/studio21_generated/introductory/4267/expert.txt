 In Dark Souls, players level up trading souls for stats. 8 stats are upgradable this way: vitality, attunement, endurance, strength, dexterity, resistance, intelligence, and faith. Each level corresponds to adding one point to a stat of the player's choice. Also, there are 10 possible classes each having their own starting level and stats:     Warrior     (Level 4):  11, 8, 12, 13, 13, 11, 9, 9 Knight
