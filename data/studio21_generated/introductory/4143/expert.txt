 [Generala](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generala) is a dice game popular in South America. It's very similar to [Yahtzee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yahtzee) but with a different scoring approach. It is played with 5 dice, and the possible results are: Generala: 50 Poker: 40 Fullhouse: 30 Straight: 20 Anything else: 0 Complete the function that is given the rolled dice as a string of length `5` and return the points scored in that roll. You can safely assume that provided parameters will be valid:  * String of length 5
