If a locker is closed when Johnny gets to it, he opens it, and vice versa. The lockers are numbered sequentially, starting at 1. Starting at the first locker, Johnny runs down the row, opening each locker. Then he runs all the way back to the beginning and runs down the row again, this time skipping to every other locker.
