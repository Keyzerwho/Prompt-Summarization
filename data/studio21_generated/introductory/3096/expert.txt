 You are given a sequence of integers.  Each integer is followed by a delimiter, which tells you what to do with that integer.  The delimiters are as follows: 1: do nothing 2: swap the integer with the next integer 3: swap the integer with the previous integer 4: swap the integer with the integer following the delimiter 5: swap the integer with the integer preceding the delimiter 6: swap the integer with the integer following the integer following the delimiter 7: swap the integer with the integer preceding the integer preceding the delimiter
