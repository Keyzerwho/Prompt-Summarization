 # Task  This is one more story about our old friend, the Despotic King. # 
-----Input:----- The first line contains a single integer $N$ indicating the number of merchants. The next $N$ lines (line $2,...,N+1$) describe the wealth of these $N$ merchants. Line $i+1$ contains a single positive integer indicating the wealth of the $i^{th}$ merchant to enter the hall. -----Output:----- Your output should consist of $N$ lines. Line $i$ should be the rank announced when the $i^{th}$ minister enters the hall. -----Constraints:----- - $1 \leq N \leq 45000$. - No two merchants have the same wealth. - You may also assume that in $30 \%$ of of the inputs $1 \leq N \leq 8000$. -----Sample Input----- 6 78 24 68 40 39 89 -----Sample Output----- 1 2 2 3 4 1
