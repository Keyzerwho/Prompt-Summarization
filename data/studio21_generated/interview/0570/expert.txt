 You are given a sequence of characters. Your challenge is to generate all possible sequences such that these phrases don't occur. 
-----Input:----- - First line will contain $T$, number of testcases. Then the testcases follow.  - Each line consists of a string $S$ (3<=s.length<=18) of distinct characters. -----Output:----- Print the number of sequences that can be formed by permuting all the characters such that phrases "kar" and "shi" don't occur. -----Constraints----- - $1 \leq T \leq 10$ - $3 \leq S.length \leq 18$ -----Sample Input:----- 2 karp abcd -----Sample Output:----- 22 24
