 There are n people and 40 types of hats labeled from 1 to 40. Given a list of list of integers hats, where hats[i] is a list of all hats preferred by the i-th person. Return the number of ways that the n people wear different hats to each other. Since the answer may be too large, return it modulo 10^9 + 7.   Example 1: Input: hats = [[3,4],[4,5],[5]] Output: 1 Explanation: There is only one way to choose hats given the conditions.
