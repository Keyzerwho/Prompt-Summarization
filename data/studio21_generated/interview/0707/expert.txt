 Anshu climbed the mountain from left to right. He remembered the number of stones he saw at each level. He forgot the number at the top. Can you help him? 
-----Input----- - First line contains T the number of test cases. - First line of each test test case contains N. - Next N-1 lines contains X and Y the stones which are connected. -----Output----- - Output the required non decreasing sequence. -----Constraints and Subtasks----- - 1 <= T <= 10 - 1 <= X, Y <= N Subtask 1: 20 points - 3<=N<=100 Subtask 2: 30 points - 3<=N<=10000 Subtask 3: 50 points - 3<=N<=100000 -----Example----- Input: 1 5 1 2 1 3 2 4 2 5 Output: 1 2 3 4 5
