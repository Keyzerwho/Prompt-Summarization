 Given an array of citations (each citation is a non-negative integer) of a researcher, write a function to compute the researcher's h-index. According to the definition of h-index on Wikipedia: "A scientist has index h if h of his/her N papers have at least h citations each, and the other N   h papers have no more than h citations each." Example: Input: citations = [3,0,6,1,5] Output: 3  Explanation: [3,0,6,1,5] means the researcher has 5 papers
