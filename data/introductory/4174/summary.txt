Write a function smallest that will find the smallest positive number that is evenly divisible by all of the numbers from 1 to n. n <= 40. 

Examples:
Input:
smallest(5)
Output:
60
Explanation:
1 to 5 can all divide evenly into 60

Input:
smallest(10)
Output:
2520
Explanation:
2520 is the smallest number that can be divided by each of the numbers from 1 to 10 without any remainder.
