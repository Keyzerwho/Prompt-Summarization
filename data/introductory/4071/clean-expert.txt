Given a year, find the next happy year or the closest year you'll see your best friend. Input Year with in range 1000  ≤  y  ≤  9000. Examples: Input: nextHappyYear(7712) Output: 7801 Explanation: As the next closest year with only distinct digits is 7801. Input: nextHappyYear(8989) Output: 9012 Explanation: As the next closest year with only distinct digits is 9012. Input: nextHappyYear(1001) Output: 1023 Explanation: As the next closest year with only distinct digits is 1023.
