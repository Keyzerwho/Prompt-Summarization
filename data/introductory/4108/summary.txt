Write a function named sumEvenNumbers, taking a sequence of numbers as single parameter. Your function must return the sum of the even values of this sequence. Only numbers without decimals like 4 or 4.0 can be even. 

Input:
* sequence of numbers: those numbers could be integers and/or floats. 

Example:
Input:
[4,3,1,2,5,10,6,7,9,8]
Output:
30
Explanation:
4 + 2 + 10 + 6 + 8 = 30
