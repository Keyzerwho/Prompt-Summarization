=====Problem Statement=====
Given a string, capitalize each word by making the first letter of each word uppercase. 

For example:
alison heck => Alison Heck

Given a string, your task is to capitalize the words appropriately.

=====Input Format=====
A single line of input containing a string, S.

=====Constraints=====
0<len(S)<1000
The string consists of alphanumeric characters and spaces.

=====Output Format=====
Print the capitalized string, S.
