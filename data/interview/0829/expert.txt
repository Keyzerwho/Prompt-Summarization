
You are given N teams which can play with each exactly one time.Each team is assigned a strength as a positive integer.Find the sum of all the absolute difference between the strengths of all the possible two matches.


-----Input format-----
Line 1 : A single integer, N.
Line 2 : N space-separated integers, the strengths of the N teams.

-----Output format-----
A single integer, total of absolute difference between the strengths of all the possible two matches.

-----Sample input-----
4
3 10 3 5

-----Sample output-----
23
