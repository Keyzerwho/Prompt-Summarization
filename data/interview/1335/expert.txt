You are given an array $a$ of item of size $N$.Only one type of item can be chosen in a day and purchased at most 2 items of that type.Find minimum number of days in which all items are purchased.

-----Input:-----
- First-line will contain $N$, the number of item.  
- The next line will contain $N$ space integers denoting the type of item.

-----Output:-----
Output the minimum number of days in which he can eat all the sweets.

-----Constraints-----
- $1 \leq N \leq 10000$
- $1 \leq a[i] \leq 10^3$

-----Sample Input:-----
3
1 2 2

-----Sample Output:-----
2
