You are given $R$ rows and $C$ columns of a matrix. Calculate the total number in the matrix. 

-----Input:-----
- First-line will contain $T$, the number of test cases. Then the test cases follow. 
- Each test case contains a single line of input, two integers $R$ and $C$. 

-----Output:-----
For each test case, output number of cupcakes you have to make.

-----Constraints-----
- $1 \leq T \leq 1000$
- $2 \leq R,C \leq 10^6$

-----Sample Input:-----
1
5 10

-----Sample Output:-----
50
