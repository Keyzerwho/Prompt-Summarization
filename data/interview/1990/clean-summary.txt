You are given n pairs of numbers where the first number is always smaller than the second number. A pair (c, d) can follow another pair (a, b) if and only if b < c.Chain can be formed in such a fashion. Find the length of longest chain which can be formed. Example 1: Input: [[1,2], [2,3], [3,4]] Output: 2 Explanation: The longest chain is [1,2] -> [3,4]
